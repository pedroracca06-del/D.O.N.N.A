## Bias Shift / Structural Invalidation

When structure shifts mid-setup, the original directional bias is abandoned:

> "705 gets hit and then we dump off this. So what am I looking for now? I'm looking for the low to get taken because we shifted structure."

A structure shift during a bullish setup (e.g., price dumps aggressively from 0.705) signals that the bias has flipped to bearish. The prior long setup is invalidated. A new short setup may form if a fresh manipulation leg and OTE can be identified.
